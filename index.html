About Us

TONG XIN HE COMPANY, with more than a decade professional working experiences, specially focuses on plywood products manufacturing and international sales. Our two production bases are located in Xuzhou and Linyi city which is the largest plywood processing base in China.

We specialize in producing film faced plywood, commercial plywood ,Melamine faced MDF, etc. Our products are certified by CE, CARB P2, and our management system are certified by ISO 9001 and ISO14001, all the products are exported to European countries, American, Southeast Asia countries.
